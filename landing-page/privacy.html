Purposa strives for transparency and trust when it comes to protecting your privacy and we aim to clearly explain how we collect and process your information. This privacy policy will explain how Purposa uses the personal data we may collect from you when you use this website.

As our application and services evolve so we will continue to update our privacy policy and terms of use. This version is published 28th May 2020.

You have the right to access, change or delete your personal information at any time. You can get in touch with us about data privacy at hey@purposa.co

What data do we collect?

Purposa collects all or some of the following:

  • Personal identification information, including your name, email address, phone number, social media accounts.
  • For job seeking candidates we may collect your work and education history e.g. job titles, company names and dates, and job preferences e.g. salary and location.
  • You may also submit information about your personality, working style and values, and causes and issues of interest or you are engaged with.
  • Note that you choose what information to add, include or display on your profile, such as your education, work experience, skills, photo, video, qualities, and location. Please do not post or add personal data to your profile that you would not want to be publicly available.
  • For hiring managers, recruiters and other employer representatives we may collect your job title, company name and related information.

How do we collect your data?

You directly provide Purposa with most of the data we collect. We collect data and process data when you:

  • Submit your email address or register on our website or app.
  • Complete a survey, quiz or questionnaire or provide feedback on our website, in a social channel or via email.
  • Use or view our website via your browser’s cookies.
  • Apply for a job or connect with a hiring company when logged in.
  • Submitting job or company information to the website - directly or via a feed.

Purposa may also receive your data indirectly from the following sources:

  • Social media accounts that you grant permission for Purposa to connect to.
  • Google analytics and other analytics services. Note this is anonymous.

How will we use your data?

Purposa collects your data so that we can:

  • Connect candidates to suitable jobs and companies.
  • Enable candidates and recruiting companies to securely log in and manage their profile and actions.
  • Email or notify candidates with information about jobs, companies, news and offers we think are relevant and useful.
  • Email or notify recruiting companies or hiring managers with information about candidates and job applicants.

If you agree, we will share your data with partner companies so that they may offer you their services for example:

  • Companies advertising jobs or seeking to hire.
  • Recruitment service providers.
  • Career and personal development service providers.

How do we store your data?

Purposa stores your data with Mailchimp that complies fully with legal requirements and maintains robust security measures. For more information https://mailchimp.com/about/security/

Purposa will keep your data for up to 24 months after your last interaction in consideration of job search, hiring, onboarding and turnover cycles. Once this time period has expired without any further interaction, we will delete your data.

Marketing

Purposa would like to send you information about products and services of ours that we think you might like, as well as those of our partner companies.

  • Companies advertising jobs or seeking to hire.
  • Recruitment service providers.
  • Career and personal development service providers.

If you have agreed to receive marketing, you may always opt out at a later date.

You have the right at any time to stop Purposa from contacting you for marketing purposes or giving your data to others. If you no longer wish to be contacted for marketing purposes, please notify hey@purposa.co.

What are your data protection rights?

Purposa would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

  • The right to access – You have the right to request Purposa for copies of your personal data. We may charge you a small fee for this service.
  • The right to rectification – You have the right to request that Purposa correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request Purposa to complete the information you believe is incomplete.
  • The right to erasure – You have the right to request that Purposa erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to restrict processing – You have the right to request that Purposa restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to object to processing – You have the right to object to Purposa’s processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to data portability – You have the right to request that Purposa transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hey@purposa.co

What are cookies?

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information. When you visit our websites, we may collect information from you automatically through cookies or similar technology

How do we use cookies?

Purposa uses cookies in a range of ways to improve your experience on our website, including:

  • Keeping you signed in
  • Understanding how you use our website
  • Serving relevant and timely notifications, content or adverts

What types of cookies do we use?

There are a number of different types of cookies, however, our website uses:

  • Functionality – Purposa uses these cookies so that we recognize you on our website and remember your previously selected preferences. These could include what language you prefer and location you are in. A mix of first-party and third-party cookies are used.
  • Advertising – Purposa uses these cookies to collect information about your visit to our website, the content you viewed, the links you followed and information about your browser, device, and your IP address. Purposa sometimes shares some limited aspects of this data with third parties for advertising purposes. We may also share online data collected through cookies with our advertising partners. This means that when you visit another website, you may be shown advertising based on your browsing patterns on our website.

How to manage cookies

You can set your browser not to accept cookies, and the above website tells you how to remove cookies from your browser. However, in a few cases, some of our website features may not function as a result.

Privacy policies of other websites

The Purposa website contains links to other websites. Our privacy policy applies only to our website, so if you click on a link to another website, you should read their privacy policy.

Changes to our privacy policy

Purposa keeps its privacy policy under regular review and places any updates on this web page. This privacy policy was last updated on 9 January 2019.

How to contact us
