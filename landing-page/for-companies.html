About us

Hey, thank you for stopping by. We want to briefly introduce who we are, what we’re doing and why.

We're a technology company with a social impact mission to speed up meaningful change in society and help the planet - by helping responsible organisations of all kinds attract, hire and inspire with their purpose, at scale.

We think the way we find jobs and engage with companies needs a rethink. Shifts in society and the economy - amplified by the current global pandemic, technology and urgent environmental and climate issues means our perspective and priorities are changing fast.

We offer a practical solution for individuals and organisations to better connect, on more human terms, and deliver on impact goals as an integral part of their daily working lives. From zero carbon to universal mental healthcare, unbounded diversity and inclusion, fair tax and open and transparent corporate management.

If this resonates you’re not alone. Our research shows the majority of working age adults now consider company reputation and its impact to society and the planet *before* applying for a job - even in these uncertain times. This is further magnified for both younger and more senior groups.

We're a small team of experts in talent and HR, organisational psychology, ESG (environmental, social and governance) and creating great digital experiences. We’re in stealth mode as we prepare to launch, gaining good momentum with organisations, partners and investors.
